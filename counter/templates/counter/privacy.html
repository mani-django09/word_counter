Privacy Policy

How we protect your privacy and handle your data at Word Counter Pro.

Last Updated: January 2025

Privacy Promise

Your text is completely private. All processing happens locally in your browser. We never see, store, or transmit your content. This isn't just a policyâ€”it's how our technology works.

1. Information We Don't Collect

Your Text Content: We never receive, store, or process your text on our servers. All analysis happens in your browser.

Personal Information: We don't collect names, email addresses, or personal details unless you contact us directly.

Files: Uploaded files are processed locally and never transmitted to our servers.

Account Data: We don't require accounts or user registration.

2. Information We May Collect

Anonymous Analytics: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our service, including:

  • Page views and tool usage frequency
  • Browser type and operating system (anonymized)
  • General geographic location (country level only)
  • Performance metrics to optimize loading times

Technical Data: Standard web server logs may include IP addresses, timestamps, and error logs for security and maintenance purposes.

3. How Our Technology Protects You

Client-Side Processing: All text analysis algorithms run in your browser using JavaScript. Your text never leaves your device.

No Server Communication: Our tools don't send your content to any external servers for processing.

Local Storage Only: Any temporary data is stored locally in your browser and automatically cleared when you close the tab.

Secure Connection: All communication with our servers uses HTTPS encryption, though no content data is transmitted.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

We use minimal cookies and local storage for:

  • Remembering your theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Storing tool preferences and settings
  • Anonymous analytics (if enabled)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting the core functionality of our tools.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics (Google Analytics with IP anonymization)
  • CDN: Content delivery for faster loading (Cloudflare)
  • Hosting: Website hosting services

These services do not have access to your text content as it's processed entirely in your browser.

6. Data Retention

Content Data: Zero retention. We never receive your content.

Analytics Data: Anonymous usage data is retained for up to 26 months for service improvement.

Server Logs: Technical logs are retained for 30 days for security purposes.

Contact Information: If you email us, we retain correspondence for up to 2 years for support purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what data we collect (detailed in this policy)
  • Request deletion of any personal data we may have
  • Opt out of analytics tracking
  • Access any personal information we hold
  • Use our service without providing any personal information

8. Security Measures

We implement various security measures:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Minimal data collection to reduce risk
  • Secure hosting infrastructure
  • No database storage of user content

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is safe for all ages. We don't knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since we don't require registration and process everything locally, children can use our tools safely.

10. International Users

Word Counter Pro is available worldwide. Since processing happens in your browser, your data stays in your country. Our servers are located in the United States, but they only serve the application code, not your content.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our service constitutes acceptance of any changes.

12. Contact Us

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us:

  • Email: privacy@wordcounter3.com
  • Subject Line: Privacy Policy Question

We typically respond within 48 hours and are committed to addressing all privacy concerns promptly.
