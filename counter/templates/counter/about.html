About Word Counter Pro

Professional text analysis tools built for writers, students, and content creators worldwide.

Our Mission

To provide fast, accurate, and privacy-focused text analysis tools that help writers, students, and professionals create better content. We believe in making powerful writing tools accessible to everyone, completely free.

Our Story

Word Counter Pro was created from a simple need: reliable, fast, and private text analysis tools. As writers and content creators ourselves, we were frustrated with existing tools that were either slow, inaccurate, or compromised user privacy.

We built Word Counter Pro with a focus on three core principles: speed, accuracy, and privacy. All text processing happens locally in your browser, ensuring your content remains completely private while delivering lightning-fast results.

What Makes Us Different

Privacy First

Your text never leaves your device. All analysis happens locally in your browser, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Lightning Fast

Real-time analysis as you type. No waiting, no delays - get instant results for all your text analysis needs.

Comprehensive Tools

Beyond basic word counting - reading time, keyword density, text cleaning, case conversion, and more.

Always Free

No subscriptions, no limits, no hidden fees. All features are completely free for everyone to use.
