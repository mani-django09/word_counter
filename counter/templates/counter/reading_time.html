Content Length Guidelines

Blog Posts

Quick read: 300-600 words (1-3 min)
Medium post: 1,000-1,500 words (4-7 min)
In-depth article: 2,000+ words (9+ min)
Optimal length: 1,600 words (7 min)

Presentations

Elevator pitch: 75-100 words (30 sec)
5-minute talk: 650-750 words
10-minute talk: 1,300-1,500 words
20-minute talk: 2,600-3,000 words

Social Media

Twitter thread: 50-100 words (30 sec)
LinkedIn post: 150-300 words (1-2 min)
Facebook post: 100-250 words (1 min)
Instagram caption: 125-150 words (30-45 sec)

Academic Content

Abstract: 150-300 words (1-2 min)
Essay (undergrad): 1,500-2,500 words (7-11 min)
Research paper: 3,000-8,000 words (13-36 min)
Thesis chapter: 8,000-10,000 words (36-45 min)
