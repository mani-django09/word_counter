Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a bug?

Email us at bugs@wordcounter3.com with details about the issue, including your browser type, operating system, and steps to reproduce the problem.

Is Word Counter Pro really free?

Yes, all features are completely free with no hidden costs, subscriptions, or premium tiers. We believe everyone should have access to quality writing tools.

Do you store my text?

No, we never store your text. All processing happens locally in your browser, ensuring complete privacy and security of your content.

Can I use this for commercial projects?

Absolutely! Word Counter Pro is free for both personal and commercial use. No attribution required.

Response Time

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours. For urgent issues, please mention "URGENT" in your subject line.
